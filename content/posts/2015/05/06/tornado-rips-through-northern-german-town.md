A violent tornado has caused extensive damage in the northern German town of Buetzow.

Five container ships broke loose from their moorings and collided.

At least 30 others were injured.

Bremen recorded a wind gust of 101kph, with wind gusts in Broken touching 108kph.

The average is nearer 16C.

Laage, which is around 23km to the southeast of Rostock, recorded 84mm of rain on Tuesday afternoon.
