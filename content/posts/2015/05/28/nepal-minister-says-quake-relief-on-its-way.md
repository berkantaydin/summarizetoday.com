A month after the earthquake that killed at least 8,600 people, Nepal's information minister, Minendra Rijal, that help is on its way to those affected.

He told Al Jazeera's Harry Fawcett that people in need across the country should be getting money and temporary shelters in time for the approaching monsoon season.
