Pakistan helicopter carrying foreign diplomats crashes

Date Fri 08 May 2015 Tags aljazeera

A Pakistani military helicopter carrying foreign diplomats in the mountainous region of Gilgit-Baltistan has crashed, killing at four people, the military says.

The aircraft crashed in the Naltar Valley area of Gilgit-Baltistan, about 300km north of the capital Islamabad.

A witness to the crash told Al Jazeera that it appeared that aircraft had suffered a technical issue.
